Personal Update - "Time to Go"

Last Updated: 14th Dec 2021

In the past decade+, I have gone through multiple (overlapping) ~5 year stretches. One of them was crisp (formerly Tenreads) the startup I worked with right after my UG from SASTRA University. The people and the experience molded me as a person.

The other one close to my heart (literally) was this long distance relationship. And last month, we called it quits. This experience also shaped me in ways I can't explain easily.

Post that, I took a break from office in the first 14 days of December & attended a bunch of weddings some of whom are very close friends. Upon reflecting & speaking to my mentors, my parents, and also my friends - I don't see any point in wallowing at this point. It's like listening to a single line of a song, on repeat (not the whole song).

And that brought me to Phil Jackson's infamous words after the Chicago Bulls' 2nd 3-peat in 1998 (from the Last Dance) - Time to Go.

* CUE SONG *

Do you see the way that tree bends?

Does it inspire?

Leaning out to catch the sun's rays...

a lesson to be applied...

Are you gettin' something out of this all encompassing trip?

You can spend your time alone redigesting past regrets oh...

Or you can come to terms and realize

You're the only one who can forgive yourself oh yeah...

Makes much more sense to live in the present tense...

Grateful for what happened. Time to Go.
